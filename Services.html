MUMBAI

Established in the bustling neighborhood of Mumbai's Dadar in 1988, our tile manufacturing site has been a cornerstone of quality and innovation for over three decades. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a leading provider of premium tiles, serving the diverse needs of homeowners, architects, and designers alike.

SURAT

In 1990, we embarked on an exciting new chapter in our journey by opening our second tile manufacturing site in the bustling city of Surat, Gujarat. Building upon the success and expertise gained from our Mumbai operations, this expansion marked a significant milestone in our growth trajectory. Situated in the heart of Gujarat's industrial hub, our Surat facility quickly became a beacon of quality and innovation in the region, catering to the burgeoning demand for premium tiles.

CHENNAI

Five years later, in a strategic move to further expand our reach and cater to the growing demand for premium tiles, we proudly inaugurated our third manufacturing site in the vibrant city of Chennai. Established with the same dedication to quality and innovation that defines our brand, this new facility represented a significant step forward in our mission to provide exceptional tiling solutions across India.
